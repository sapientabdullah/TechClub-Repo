  • Co-founded X.com, an online financial services and e-mail payment company, with US$10 million from the sale of Zip2.

  • With US$100 million of his early fortune, Musk founded Space Exploration Technologies.

10 February 2006

  • Musk provided the initial concept, and financial capital for Solar City, co-founded by his cousins Lyndon and Peter Rive.
